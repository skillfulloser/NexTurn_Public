NexTurn

Your board game life, made easier. Turn order tracking, game session management, and tournament hosting — all in one sleek device and companion app.

App Features

🎲

Game Session Tracking

Track every game you play. See detailed stats by player, game, and time period. Push sessions directly to your BGG profile.

🎨

Custom Player Colors

Set custom colors per player, save color palettes for your favorite games, and see your seating arrangement on a virtual table.

⚔️

RPG Mode

Specify any number of players, enemies, NPCs, and environments with custom initiative tracking and combat round management.

🏆

Tournaments

Swiss, Round Robin, Elimination, Raising Stakes, and more. Full bracket management with standings, scoring, and multi-device support.

📊

Statistics & Streaks

Player count stats, turn time analysis, win tracking, leaderboards, and achievement streaks across all your sessions.

📱

Device Mode or Phone

Use NexTurn devices for an immersive experience, or run everything from your phone. The app works standalone without devices.

Turn Order Modes

Clockwise Classic turn order, clockwise or counter-clockwise around the table.
Manual Pick a specific turn order. Can be updated between rounds.
Race Players race to be the first to end their turn.
Random After a turn is finished, the next player is chosen randomly.
Steal Combine with any mode for games with steal or follow actions — like Glory to Rome or Mahjong.
RPG Full RPG initiative tracking with drag-to-reorder, combat rounds, and admin phases.
