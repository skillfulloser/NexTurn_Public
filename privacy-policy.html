Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Introduction

Drop the Dice LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates the NexTurn mobile application and NexTurn hardware devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using NexTurn, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name / username
  • Authentication credentials (managed securely through AWS Cognito)

Game Session Data

  • Game names, player names, turn times, and session statistics
  • Tournament configurations and results
  • Color palettes and game preferences

Device Information

  • Push notification tokens (for friend and tournament notifications)
  • Device identifiers (for NexTurn hardware pairing only)

Tournament Data

  • If a tournament organizer uploads a CSV of participants, that file may contain names, email addresses, and seeding information. This data is stored solely for tournament management purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the NexTurn app and device functionality
  • Sync game sessions across your devices
  • Enable social features (friends, pending plays, tournament notifications)
  • Display global game statistics and leaderboards
  • Improve our services based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

Third-Party Integrations

NexTurn integrates with BoardGameGeek (BGG) at your request. When you connect your BGG account and choose to log a play, session data (game name, date, player names, scores, location) is sent to BGG on your behalf. We do not store your BGG credentials — authentication is handled through secure OAuth-style sessions.

We do not sell, share, or provide your personal data to any other third-party services, vendors, or companies.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Amazon Web Services (AWS), including DynamoDB for session data and Cognito for authentication. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

NexTurn hardware devices do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. They receive only lighting commands (color, pattern) via wireless connection.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at contact@nexturn.app. Upon request, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Children's Privacy

NexTurn is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contact@nexturn.app and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of NexTurn after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at contact@nexturn.app.
